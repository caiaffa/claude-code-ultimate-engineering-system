# PRD_QUESTIONS

## Problem validation
- What metric shows this problem is real?
- What is the baseline today?
- How large is the impact?
- Are we solving root cause or symptom?
- What happens if we do nothing?

## Value and gain
- What business metric should improve?
- What gain do we expect?
- In what time horizon?
- What mechanism links the solution to the expected gain?
- Is the gain large enough to justify cost and complexity?

## Scope discipline
- What is true MVP?
- What could be deferred without losing most of the value?
- What is included that does not materially change the outcome?
- Are there hidden operational or support requirements?

## Delivery and operation
- Who owns this after launch?
- How does it fail?
- How is failure detected?
- How is rollback done?
- What data, observability, security, and support work is required?

## Measurement
- What are the primary, secondary, and guardrail metrics?
- When will we measure?
- What result counts as success?
- What result means the hypothesis was wrong?
