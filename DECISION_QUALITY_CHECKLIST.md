# DECISION_QUALITY_CHECKLIST

- [ ] Problem clearly stated
- [ ] Evidence exists for why the problem matters
- [ ] Constraints are explicit
- [ ] Alternatives are real and honestly evaluated
- [ ] Business impact is measurable
- [ ] Trade-offs are explicit
- [ ] Risks are concrete
- [ ] Reversibility is addressed
- [ ] Rollout and rollback are credible
- [ ] Success criteria are measurable
- [ ] If wrong, we will know how and when
