# PRD_REVIEW_TEMPLATE

## Problem
What business problem is being solved?

## Evidence
What metrics or evidence show this is a real problem today?

## Baseline
What is the current baseline for the relevant metrics?

## Affected users or operations
Who is impacted, and how?

## Goal
What outcome do we want?

## Non-goals
What is intentionally out of scope?

## Primary metric
What is the main success metric?

## Secondary metrics
What supporting metrics will be observed?

## Guardrail metrics
What must not get worse?

## Hypothesis of impact
What improvement do we expect, by what mechanism, and within what time frame?

## Why now
Why should this be done now?

## Alternatives
What simpler or cheaper alternatives exist?

## Cost
What is the expected implementation and operating cost?

## Risks
What technical, operational, legal, support, or data risks exist?

## Rollout and measurement plan
How will we launch and measure post-release impact?

## Decision rule
Under what conditions would we stop, scale, or revise this initiative?
