# ADR_TEMPLATE

## Title
Short, decision-grade title.

## Status
Proposed | Accepted | Superseded | Rejected

## Date
YYYY-MM-DD

## Owners
Decision owner(s)

## Context
What is happening now? What technical or business situation makes this decision necessary?

## Problem
What exact problem are we solving? Be precise. Separate root problem from symptoms.

## Business relevance
Why does this matter now?
- affected users or operations
- business risk
- cost of inaction
- urgency or opportunity window

## Constraints
List hard constraints:
- time
- compatibility
- regulatory or security
- infra/runtime limitations
- team capacity
- migration limits

## Options considered
For each option:
- summary
- benefits
- drawbacks
- operational impact
- security impact
- observability impact
- cost impact
- reversibility

## Decision
What are we choosing?

## Why this option
Why is this the best choice under current constraints?

## Trade-offs accepted
What are we intentionally giving up?

## Risks
What can go wrong?
- technical
- operational
- compatibility
- delivery
- human/process

## Failure modes
What are the most important ways this decision can fail in production?

## Rollout and migration plan
How will this be introduced safely?

## Rollback and reversibility
How do we back out if needed? What is irreversible?

## Success criteria
What evidence would show this decision worked?

## Open questions
What remains uncertain?
