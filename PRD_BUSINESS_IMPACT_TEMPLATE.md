# PRD_BUSINESS_IMPACT_TEMPLATE

## Problem being solved
Describe the business problem precisely.

## Current evidence and baseline
List the current metrics proving the problem exists.
- metric name
- current value
- time window
- source

## Affected users or operations
Who feels this problem today?

## Primary metric
What is the main metric to improve?

## Secondary metrics
What additional metrics help confirm impact?

## Guardrail metrics
What must not worsen?

## Expected gain
What specific gain do we expect?
- absolute or percentage improvement
- expected time to observe
- mechanism of improvement

## Cost to build
Estimated implementation cost.

## Cost to operate
Expected ongoing operational cost.

## Risks and trade-offs
What could worsen? What complexity are we adding?

## Why now
Why is this worth doing now?

## Why this solution
Why this approach instead of simpler alternatives?

## Why not do nothing
What is the cost of inaction?

## Decision if impact is not observed
What will we do if the expected gain does not materialize?
