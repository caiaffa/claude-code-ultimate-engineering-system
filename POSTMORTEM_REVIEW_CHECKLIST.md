# POSTMORTEM_REVIEW_CHECKLIST

## Root cause depth
- [ ] The document separates symptom, trigger, local cause, and systemic cause
- [ ] It explains why the failure was possible
- [ ] It explains why the failure was not detected earlier
- [ ] It explains why containment was weak or slow

## Quality of learning
- [ ] It avoids blame as a substitute for mechanism
- [ ] It identifies missing safeguards
- [ ] It identifies missing observability
- [ ] It identifies missing ownership or process clarity
- [ ] It proposes structural improvements, not only local fixes

## Corrective actions
- [ ] Actions have owners
- [ ] Actions have deadlines
- [ ] Actions reduce recurrence risk materially
- [ ] Actions improve detection or containment
- [ ] Actions map to standards, templates, or guardrails when appropriate
